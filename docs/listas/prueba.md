The '''Bear Village''', (formerly known or also known as '''The Hub''') is the main area in [[Super Bear Adventure (game)|'''Super Bear Adventure''']]. As the name suggests, the village inhabits lots of [[bears]], but there are also other non-bear animals residing in the village. The Bear Village leads you to [[Turtletown]], [[Snow Valley]], [[Beemothep Desert]], [[Giant House]] and [[The Hive (disambiguation)|The Hive]]. It also introduces certain mechanics to players. The Bear Village has changed multiple times across the updates.

== History ==

=== Before the events of the game ===
The kingdom was once a very peaceful place, under the rule of the Bee Dynasty, and their ruler, [[Queen Beeatrice]].

[[Baaren's Brother|Baaren's brother]] has requested [[Baaren]] to be his assistant in conquering the kingdom together. Baaren refused, which made his brother upset. He plans to have revenge on his brother by invading the kingdom himself without his assistance.

A few moments after, Baaren's brother stole the Kernel from an unsuspecting [[Scientist Bear|scientist]]. After that, he makes a [[Baaren's brother's hideout|secret hideout]] mainly for experiments on [[Purple Honey|purple honey]], a new substance he also stole from the [[Scientist Bear|scientist]]. He stayed in there for a long time. One day, he experimented on his younger brother, which caused <abbr>him</abbr> to be forgetful. As the days passed, his fur turned completely purple due to the repeated experiments of himself. Eventually, fearing suspicion from his fellow bears, he decided to leave the [[The Hub|Bear Village]].

=== Story ===
One day, Baaren's brother has executed his plan by kidnapping the queen to prevent her to stop his plan entirely, and making the bees as his servants and to order them to create [[Purple Honey|purple honey]] specifically to target the inhabitants to prevent them to fight back from the perilous invasion. Some inhabitants already consumed the substance, which led them to become mindless creatures, which infected other animals. After this, there are little survivors left from the purple honey. (Which includes YOU or the player)

The bees found out that the [[bears]] were unaffected by the purple honey. The bees realized that with the bears immune to purple honey, they might form a rebellion group against the invasion, and they are worried that they might ruin their [[Baaren's Brother|leader's]] plan. And so they raided the Bear Village and imprisoned the bears, even the baby bears, even though they don't know what to do, or understand the concept of rebellion.

Baaren managed to dodge the invasion, simply by sleeping on a tree, unaware of what is happening. But he woke up to unknown causes, and he found out that his home is in shambles. He knew he needed more help and so he found a Backpacker named [[Shicka]]. And then they ran off to save the bears and save the kingdom on their own.

== Appearance ==
The Bear Village is a grassy area with grass paths all over. The main and uppermost area of the Bear Village is divided into two sections, divided by a river coming from the Snow Valley entrance. The lowermost area has a pond which ends the river. There is an abundant number of trees and bear houses.

After beating the game, The Bear Village gets a more vibrant and colorful vibe, as the [[Queen Beeatrice|Queen]] organized a party. The Hive entrance is now closed and sealed shut with wooden slabs. There are loads of confetti lying around, except in some parts like the end of the river and the level entrances. They are also accompanied by colorful pennant banners hanging against walls. There are also various inhabitants in the villages, including bees.

The further you progress in the game, the less purple honey there is to be found.

==Locations and Parts==

===Main===
[[https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/3d-platformer-super-bear-adventure/images/f/f1/Spawnpoint.png/revision/latest|thumb|left|206x206px]]
'''Spawnpoint'''<br>
This is where you spawn after you open your save or you go back to the Bear Village after visiting a level. Instantly, you will see the [[Capitalus Brothers]] and their respective shop stands in front of you. You spawn on a grass path shaped like a circle, which is the center of the Bear Village.

{{clear}}
[[https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/3d-platformer-super-bear-adventure/images/1/18/Outfitshopplatform.png/revision/latest|thumb|left|206x206px]]
'''[[Outfit Shop]]'''<br>
This is where you can change your character's outfit. Upon stepping on the stone platform, the HUD showing the outfit options will automatically appear and [[Capitalus]] will come to you to assist and watch you change outfits. When finished, Capitalus will go back to his shop stand.

{{clear}}
[[https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/3d-platformer-super-bear-adventure/images/b/ba/River.png/revision/latest|thumb|left|206x206px]]
'''River'''<br>
This divides the upper area of the Bear Village into two parts. The water comes from a frozen river in the [[Snow Valley]] entrance area, which melted from ice to water, creating a flowing river.

{{clear}}
[[https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/3d-platformer-super-bear-adventure/images/2/22/Lowerarea.png/revision/latest|thumb|left|206x206px]]
'''Lower area'''<br>
The river ends in this area of the Bear Village, which is the lowermost part. It contains a turtle house, the entrance area to the [[Arcade World]], the [[DJ Pierre]] club and the [[Soccer/Football Field]], and vines which you can climb up to the upper area.

{{clear}}
[[https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/3d-platformer-super-bear-adventure/images/2/28/Tutorialzone.png/revision/latest|thumb|left|206x206px]]
'''Tutorial Zone'''<br>
This is where you spawn when starting a new save. This area is the training grounds for learning the [[Controls and Moveset|basic controls and moveset]] (which includes swimming, rotating the camera, and picking up objects). The upper part of the tutorial zone teaches you more complex moves such as, the stomp boost move and the double dive move.

{{clear}}
[[https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/3d-platformer-super-bear-adventure/images/4/45/Tristopioshop.png/revision/latest|thumb|left|206x206px]]
'''[[Tristopio]]'s shop'''<br>
Beside the start of the river, this is where [[Tristopio]] resides with his shop stand. He is also beside the [[Beemothep Desert]]'s entrance area.

{{clear}}
[[https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/3d-platformer-super-bear-adventure/images/5/54/Darkwatercave.png/revision/latest|thumb|left|206x206px]]
'''Dark water cave'''<br>
This intersects the main [[Snow Valley]] area and the spot above [[Tristopio]]'s shop, and beside the river. This is also where you can find [[Baaren's brother's hideout|Baaren's brother hideout]], as there is a small hole in the water that leads to it. It is a dark cave containing water and a large soccer ball/football.

===Level entrance areas===

{{clear}}
[[https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/3d-platformer-super-bear-adventure/images/c/cb/Turtletownentrancearea.png/revision/latest|thumb|left|206x206px]]
'''[[Turtletown]]'''<br>
The entrance area is out in the open; it doesn't have a respective area, unlike [[Snow Valley]], [[Beemothep Desert]], and [[The Hive (new)|The Hive]]. It is located above the lower area, above the vines.

{{clear}}
[[https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/3d-platformer-super-bear-adventure/images/9/90/Snowvalleyentrancearea.png/revision/latest|thumb|left|206x206px]]
'''[[Snow Valley]]'''<br>
On the left of the spawnpoint, there is the entrance area of Snow Valley, above vines you need to climb to get to the area. The entrance area corresponds to the level's theme; the entrance area has snow and ice there's even an Easter egg! There is a frozen ice river which melts to create the flowing river that is dividing the Bear Village into two parts. The level sign also has a theme with the level; it contains a saw that punctures through the wooden sign, the saw is from the sawmill. It also contains the dark water cave an a log cabin.

{{clear}}
[[https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/3d-platformer-super-bear-adventure/images/d/d3/Beemothepdesertentrancearea.png/revision/latest|thumb|left|206x206px]]
'''[[Beemothep Desert]]'''<br>
Beside Tristopio's shop, there is the entrance area of Beemothep Desert, which you can also see upon spawning in your save. The entrance area also corresponds to the theme of the level; it has sand and rocks. The level sign is not wooden; instead, it is a chipped off brick wall. It contains a bear statue and a desert house.

{{clear}}
[[https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/3d-platformer-super-bear-adventure/images/b/b6/Gianthouseentrancearea.png/revision/latest|thumb|left|206x206px]]
'''[[Giant House]]'''<br>
Like Turtletown, the entrance area if Giant House is also out in the open. It is located behind the spawnpoint and is near the tutorial zone. This entrance area is very unique among the others; it doesn't have a [[Teleporter Platform|teleporter platform]], instead, it has a [[The Shrinking Machine|shrinking machine]]. This also doesn't have a level sign of it's own, instead the name of the level is placed on the side of [[Scientist Bear|the scientist]]'s house, along with the number of bears you save.

{{clear}}
[[https://static.wikia.nocookie.net/3d-platformer-super-bear-adventure/images/2/2c/Newhiveentrancearea.png/revision/latest|thumb|left|206x206px]]
'''[[The Hive (new)]]'''<br>
Its entrance area is located beside the Giant House entrance area. The area has a series of [[Purple Honey|purple honey]] platforms you need to cross through to get to the teleporter platform. Underneath the purple honey platforms, there is a body of water containing stalactites, and if you fall into it, you will go back to the start.
