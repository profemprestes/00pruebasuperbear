'''Super Bear Adventure''' (formerly known or also known as '''Baaren's Adventure''', often abbreviated to '''SBA''' for short) is a French 3D platformer game. It is made and owned by [[Earthkwak Games]] and was initially released in June 10, 2017 for Android. The game started development in around November 2016. In October 01, 2020, the game was released for iOS. In the Americas, the Nintendo Switch version was released on December 12, 2023.

The player takes on the role of [[Baaren]]. a bear who supposedly escaped the bear imprisonment during the purple honey onslaught. As Baaren is the only bear standing, the player will use a combination of strategies, controls and tactics to traverse levels, free his fellow bears from cages, and defeat the culprit behind this mess.<br />

== Early development ==
In around November 2016, Stéphane Hulot started to work on the game. The game was supposed to be a project for fun.
[[File:Test_world.png|left|thumb|Early prototype of the game from December 20, 2016]]
[[File:Anotherimageoftestworld.png|left|thumb|Another image from the same day]]

The game originally used checkerboard textures and the Liberation Sans (the default Unity font) as placeholders. Baaren looked to have no [[Backpacker]] on him yet, and his eyes were farther apart than now. There were no levels or even a main area, just simple blocks to be a test area, with bright colors, and grid lines.
[[File:Unknown_world.png|legt|thumb|An image of an unknown world from December 24, 2016]]

At one point, an unknown world was created, where the walls were made of rocks, there was a river with logs passing through, platforms on the walls, and some boxes. The life meter was created at this point. This map possibly inspired [[Turtletown]]'s top area.

Originally, Shicka was planned to be a human doll, then a frog, which eventually turned into a chick. Baaren was the first ever character in the game.
[[File:Sbaearly2.png|left|thumb|Beta version of [[Turtletown]] from January 23, 2017]]

Turtletown's prototype concept had stone texture as for the walls instead of dirt, the waters looked very blue, and the trees had no leaves texture; just plain green. The only font used at the time was Liberation Sans.
[[File:old_sba.jpg|left|thumb|A more later beta version of [[Turtletown]] from April 8, 2017]]

The game used royalty-free music as placeholders for original music. Baaren's movement was very different, The Bear Village (known as Hub at the time) looked very compacted, and the only levels were [[Turtletown]] (known as Turtle Village at the time) and [[Snow Valley]]. Over time, the game got more features, such as new [[Outfit Shop|outfits]], [[SBA Gold|donor status]] (known as that at the time), and the Bear Village slowly expanded, along with new levels. Eventually, [[The Hive (old)|The Hive]] was added as a final level, to make the game a story-mode game, and not just a sandbox game.

==Story==

===Current (and rewritten) Story===
For centuries, There existed a kingdom where [[List of inhabitants|creatures]] lived in peace under the ruling of the Bee dynasty. [[Queen Beeatrice]], the bee queen, was a beloved ruler, she was fair and appreciated by all. The balance between all creatures was perfect. Until... The bees started producing a different kind of [[Purple Honey|honey]] in the [[The Hive (new)|Hive]]. Shortly after, this new flavor and this purple color were beloved by everybody. Unfortunately, creatures that consumed it soon began to turn crazy and attack their friends like mindless animals. Nobody knows why the bees started to wreak havoc upon their own kingdom, and its inhabitants were too weak to fight back. Before long, the bees realized that [[bears]] were mysteriously unaffected by the рurple honey. This resistance caused their loss: the bees raided the Bear Village and imprisoned them to prevent any revolt. Luckily, [[Baaren|one bear]] managed to avoid the attack. But now, the fate of the people is on his shoulders. The world needs a hero. Someone to restore the balance, Someone to end to this purple doom. Will you save us, Baaren?

===Old Story===
In this world, [[List of inhabitants|animals]] used to live in a community. Bees, bears, turtles, rabbits, were at peace. [[The Bee Queen]] reigned over the [[The Hub|kingdom]]. She was fair and appreciated by all. This balance was perfect, until the day [[Baaren's Brother|he]] arrived... He kidnapped the queen and impersonated her to quench his thirst for power. He ordered the bees to make his recipe of Purple Honey and spread it all over the kingdom. This purple honey caused animals that consumed it to be aggressive and maliceful. The bees quickly realized that bears were not affected by Purple Honey. This ability caused their loss: the bees imprisoned the bears in cages in order to avoid any revolts against the queen. The world needs more than ever a hero. You, [[Baaren]]. You are the only one who can help us! Find [[Shicka]] and go save all the bears trapped by the bees! The fate of the kingdom rests on your shoulders. You can do it!

==Trivia==

*At the beginning, the game used to be called "'''Baaren's Adventure'''", but Hulot mentioned it as "'''Baaren's Journey'''" in the Discord server after answering a question. He said that he changed the name to the current one because he wanted it to have more visibility to the Play Store.
*At the time of the game's production and release, Stéphane Hulot was 17.
*Many people call the game a knockoff of [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Banjo-Kazooie Banjo-Kazooie], but the dev wasn't aware of Banjo-Kazooie during the game's creation.
*This game is widely speedrunned, check the [[speedrunning]] page to learn more.
*PolariTOON, a speedrunner, was once helping in the French localization.
\*\*He also stated that he met the dev in real life. (revealed in SBA Discord)
*The dev has shown some screenshots of him testing out new fonts for the games's UI overhaul, with Milky Nice being the winner.
**The current font, Milky Nice, has it's outline system that consists of more outlines on the bottom, similar to the Clash of Clan's font outline system.
*A Japanese translation was once added in the game, but was removed since it had no active translators and it was translated only by DeepL, an online translator.
*An update is released around 3 or more days late on iOS due to the dev saying that Apple is strict.
\*The game bears strong resemblance to [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Super_Mario_64 Super Mario 64]:
**Instead of [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mario Mario], the main character is [[Baaren|a bear]].
**The [[bears]], which you save in the levels, act as the Power Stars in the game.
**Instead of paintings, there are [[Teleporter Platform|teleporter platforms]].
\*\*Baaren and Mario can both [[Controls and Moveset|long jump, dive and belly stomp]]. (belly stomp can act as ground pound)

- The official Discord server, was originally supposed to be a speedrunning group made by the community.

- A game titled "Cookie Swirl World" is notorious for copying the game's assets and replacing the player's model with a Roblox YouTuber's former player outfit, Cookie Swirl C. This copy game uses the original game's old assets and lighting, presumably before version 1.8.0, the coins are replaced with cookies, and the [[Outfit Shop]] is removed. However, this game is not official nor endorsed by Cookie Swirl C herself.
- Arabic was once an explored concept to put in the game as a language as seen in the game files, but was put down due to bugs and confusion due to Arabic being read and written from right to left.
  \*\* However, it was considered again and is planned to be added in a future update.
- According to analytics, out of the 100M players of the game, '''Indonesia''' has the most players, with 40M players.
  \*\* 14% of Indonesia's population has installed Super Bear Adventure (40M players from a country of 285M people).
