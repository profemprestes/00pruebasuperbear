# 06 - SBA Game Lore & Context

## Super Bear Adventure (SBA) Overview

**Super Bear Adventure** (also known as **Baaren's Adventure**) is a French 3D platformer game developed by Earthkwak Games. Released on June 10, 2017 for Android, later on iOS (October 1, 2020) and Nintendo Switch (December 12, 2023).

### Game Info
| Property | Details |
|----------|---------|
| **Developer** | Earthkwak Games |
| **Engine** | Unity |
| **Platforms** | Android, iOS, Nintendo Switch, Steam (planned) |
| **Genre** | Action-adventure, Platformer |
| **Mode** | Single player and Multiplayer |
| **Current Version** | 12.1.3 (as of November 2025) |
| **Downloads** | Over 100M downloads |
| **Rating** | 3+ (Android), 4+ (iOS), Everyone (Switch) |

### Story (Current Version)

For centuries, there existed a kingdom where creatures lived in peace under the ruling of the Bee dynasty. **Queen Beeatrice**, the bee queen, was a beloved ruler, fair and appreciated by all. The balance between all creatures was perfect.

**Until...** The bees started producing a different kind of honey in **The Hive**. Shortly after, this new flavor and purple color were beloved by everybody. Unfortunately, creatures that consumed it soon began to turn crazy and attack their friends like mindless animals.

Nobody knows why the bees started to wreak havoc upon their own kingdom, and its inhabitants were too weak to fight back. Before long, the bees realized that **bears were mysteriously unaffected** by the purple honey. This resistance caused their loss: the bees raided the Bear Village and imprisoned them to prevent any revolt.

Luckily, **one bear** managed to avoid the attack. But now, the fate of the people is on his shoulders. The world needs a hero. Someone to restore the balance, someone to end this purple doom.

**Will you save us, Baaren?**

## Main Characters

### 🐻 Baaren (The Hero)
- **Species:** Brown bear
- **Role:** Playable protagonist
- **Abilities:** Immune to purple honey, brave, determined
- **Mission:** Rescue friends and stop Baaren's Brother
- **Appearance:** Brown bear with yellow backpacker (Shicka)

### 🐣 Shicka (The Guide)
- **Species:** Yellow chick (peluche-like)
- **Role:** Deuteragonist, backpacker companion
- **Abilities:** Can carry up to 7 golden keys
- **Function:** Guides Baaren, gives advice, sacrifices self to protect Baaren from final laser
- **Appearance:** Small yellow bird with backpack

### 👑 Queen Beeatrice
- **Species:** Bee
- **Role:** Benevolent queen (mind-controlled)
- **Status:** Final boss (controlled by Baaren's Brother)
- **Appearance:** Large bee with crown

### 😈 Baaren's Brother (The Villain)
- **Species:** Bear (turned purple)
- **Role:** Main antagonist, final boss
- **Backstory:** Rejected by Baaren when proposing to conquer the kingdom together. Stole "The Kernel" artifact, kidnapped the Queen, and forced bees to produce Purple Honey
- **Appearance:** Purple bear (corrupted by honey experimentation on himself)
- **Location:** Secret cave in The Backrooms

### 🐰 Tristopio
- **Species:** Pink rabbit
- **Role:** Trainer, daily missions giver
- **Shop:** Tristopio's Shop (near Beemothep entrance)
- **Currency:** Tristopio Tokens (1 per day from daily missions)
- **Appearance:** Melancholic pink rabbit

### 🐻 Capitalus & Capitalos
- **Capitalus:** Owns outfit shop for changing costumes
- **Capitalos:** Sells legendary items, operates Fortune Roulette
- **Location:** Spawnpoint area (center circle)

### 🧪 The Scientist
- **Species:** Bear
- **Role:** Inventor of shrinking machine, owns Giant House level
- **Personality:** Eccentric, messy, had his Kernel stolen
- **Appearance:** Disheveled bear with lab coat

### 🐝 Maybee
- **Species:** Bee
- **Role:** Gift shop owner in The Hive
- **Personality:** Hates bears but does business with Baaren
- **Location:** The Hive

## Worlds & Levels

### 1. Bear Village (The Nexus)
- **Description:** Peaceful village with portals to worlds and shops
- **Features:** Spawnpoint, shops, river, tutorial zone
- **Unlock:** Starting area
- **Atmosphere:** Festive post-game with confetti and banners

### 2. Turtletown
- **Description:** Forested starting level with turtle-shell houses
- **Boss:** Turtle Golem
- **Features:** Waterfalls, green hills, turtle villagers
- **Unlock:** Available from start

### 3. Snow Valley
- **Description:** Frozen valley with penguins, moose, and Yeti
- **Features:** Ice, snowballs, igloos, frozen water
- **Unlock:** Requires 4 rescued bears

### 4. Beemothep Desert
- **Description:** Desert with ruins, pyramids, and fennecs
- **Boss:** The Guardian
- **Features:** Sand, ruins, statues, ancient structures
- **Unlock:** Requires 8 rescued bears

### 5. Giant House
- **Description:** Baaren is shrunk in the Scientist's messy house
- **Enemies:** Rats, cockroaches
- **Features:** Kitchen, living room, giant furniture
- **Unlock:** Requires 12 rescued bears

### 6. The Hive
- **Description:** Final industrial factory, home of Purple Honey
- **Boss:** Baaren's Brother
- **Features:** Purple honey platforms, stalactites, dangerous drops
- **Unlock:** Requires all rescued bears

### 7. Arcade World
- **Description:** Space-themed minigame world (races, parkour)
- **Features:** Arcade machines, coin collection, challenges
- **Unlock:** Requires 6 rescued bears

### 8. The Backrooms (Easter Egg)
- **Description:** Creepy secret labyrinth of yellow corridors from the 90s
- **Access:** Fall outside the map (noclip)
- **Guardian:** Shadow Bear (terrifying dark version of Baaren)
- **Appearance:** Yellow walls, fluorescent lights, endless maze

## Mechanics & Collectibles

### Coins
- **Usage:** Standard currency for cosmetics and Lootboxes
- **Appearance:** Golden spinning coins
- **Sources:** Scattered in levels, minigames, daily missions

### Purple Honey
- **Type:** Environmental trap
- **Effect:** Slows Baaren down
- **Appearance:** Purple glowing puddles
- **Locations:** The Hive, post-game Bear Village

### Keys
- **Carrier:** Shicka (carries in backpack)
- **Usage:** Open geometric bear cages
- **Limit:** 7 keys at a time
- **Appearance:** Golden keys

### Hearts
- **Effect:** Instantly heal 1 HP
- **Availability:** 3 per level
- **Appearance:** Red floating hearts

### VR Headsets
- **Usage:** Epic cosmetic item + "lives" system
- **Function:** 3 daily attempts for Daily Missions
- **Appearance:** VR headset on Baaren

### Tristopio Tokens
- **Source:** 1 per day from completing Daily Missions
- **Usage:** Buy exclusive pink clothing from Tristopio
- **Appearance:** Pink tokens

### Stickers
- **Type:** Invisible collectibles
- **Quantity:** 3 hidden per level
- **Usage:** Decorate the village
- **Appearance:** Hidden (need to find)

### Golden Bears (Trophies)
- **Source:** Speedrunning mechanic
- **Function:** Rewards for rescuing bears in record time
- **Appearance:** Golden bear statues

## Game Aesthetics

### Visual Style
- **Low-Poly/Voxel Art:** Blocky, geometric 3D models
- **Bright Colors:** Saturated, cheerful palette
- **Thick Outlines:** Similar to Clash of Clans outline system (more outlines on bottom)
- **Simple Textures:** Flat colors, minimal gradients
- **Blocky Characters:** Cubic heads, bodies, limbs

### UI Style
- **Dialog Boxes:** Game-style dialogs with character portraits
- **HUD Elements:** Coin counter, life meter, pause button
- **3D Buttons:** Press effect with shadow reduction
- **Progress Bars:** Loading animations with spinning elements

## Trivia

- Originally called "Baaren's Adventure" but changed to "Super Bear Adventure" for better Play Store visibility
- Developer Stéphane Hulot was 17 when creating the game
- Often compared to Banjo-Kazooie (but dev wasn't aware of it during creation)
- Strong resemblance to Super Mario 64:
  - Baaren = Mario (bear instead of human)
  - Bears = Power Stars (rescue instead of collect)
  - Teleporter platforms = Paintings (level entry)
  - Same moveset: long jump, dive, belly stomp (ground pound)
- Indonesia has most players (40M out of 100M total = 14% of population)
- Japanese translation was removed (only DeepL translated, no active translators)
- Arabic translation planned for future update
